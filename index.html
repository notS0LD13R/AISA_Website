ABOUT US

Aerospace Interested Students Association(AISA) is the space club of Mar Athanasius College Of Engineering, Kothamangalam.The club was formed by Miss Gadha Premdas and Mr Akhiljith A on the 21st of September 2019. The Club was officially inaugurated on the 17th of February 2022 by the most honourable and renowned Propulsion Scientist/Engineer at ISRO, Mr Kiran Mohan.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create a thriving community dedicated to the exploration and development of space and its technologies. We believe in fostering an environment that encourages innovation, collaboration, and growth for all members. By working together, we hope to push the boundaries of what is possible and make significant strides in space exploration and technology.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at AISA is to provide our members with the necessary tools and resources to develop a strong foundation for a successful career in the field of space technology. We strive to create opportunities for young minds to gain valuable exposure and guidance in pursuing their dreams.

What We Do

Learning opportunity

In this fast-paced era, it's essential for us to be aware of the different space technologies out there. There's so much to learn and see!

Outreach opportunity

Our club can organize outreach events and share our passion with the community around us in our locality.

Personal growth

We encourage our members to step outside their comfort zones. We hope to provide various events to help them develop their knowledge and confidence.

Host seminars & workshops

Different seminars and workshops on astronomy will be held in our campus to raise awareness ahout it.

Space Hacks

Our Space hackathons have enabled students to collaborate and generate innovative products and ideas that push the limits of space exploration and innovation.

Space Events

The club organizes various space-related activities such as quizzes and exhibitions providing opportunities for students to participate and engage in space-related events.
